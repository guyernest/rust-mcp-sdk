<objective>
Two small, independent hardening fixes:

GAP 3 (WR-02): `[backend].base_url` is `#[serde(default)]` (defaults to `""`) and never
validated. A typo'd or missing base_url parses + validates cleanly, then surfaces late
as an opaque `DispatchError::Connector("invalid base URL")` at dispatch time. Validate
it at config-parse time with a clear field-naming error.

GAP 5 (WR-04): `oauth_passthrough` relays client-controlled header bytes into an
operator-set `target_header`. This is intended SSO-passthrough behavior but is
undocumented. Document the trust boundary at the relay site and in the user-facing docs.

Purpose: Close two REVIEW warnings that affect OAPI-03's auth/config surface. No
behavior change for GAP 5 (docs only); GAP 3 turns a late opaque failure into an
actionable parse-time error.

Output: `ConfigValidationError::EmptyBackendBaseUrl` + the validate() check; a
trust-boundary doc-comment at `OAuthPassthroughAuth::apply` plus a docs note in the
crate READMEs.
</objective>
