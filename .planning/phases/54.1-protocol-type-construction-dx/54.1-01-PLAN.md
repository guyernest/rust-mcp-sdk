<objective>
Add constructors, Default impls, fluent builders, and #[non_exhaustive] to the three most painful protocol type modules: resources, prompts, and content. These are the types explicitly called out in the dev team feedback (v1.16->v1.20 upgrade) as causing the most friction.

Purpose: Eliminate struct literal construction from downstream users for ResourceInfo, ResourceTemplate, PromptInfo, PromptArgument, PromptMessage, Content variants, ResourceLinkContent, and Annotations. Add #[non_exhaustive] to prevent future field additions from breaking downstream.

Output: Three updated type modules with systematic DX pattern: #[non_exhaustive] + derive(Default) + ::new(required_fields) + .with_*(optional_field) on every struct. Content enum gets static construction helpers. In-file tests migrated to dogfood constructors.
</objective>
