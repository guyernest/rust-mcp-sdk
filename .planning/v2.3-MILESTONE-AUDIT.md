### Integration WARNING (non-blocking, by design)
`cargo pmcp workbook emit` (dev-only) writes `evidence/gate.json` + `evidence/gate.sha256`, which are not in the served loader's `ALLOWED_MEMBERS`. Serving an *emitted* (not *compiled*) bundle fails closed with `BundleLoadError::UnexpectedMember`. Emit prints "UNGATED — do not deploy"; the production `compile` path produces clean 7-member bundles. Recommend a one-line note in the Shape A/B serve docs. (WBCL-03 UX only.)
