<objective>
Close the SDK-side gap reported in `pmcp-run/.planning/notes/sdk-issue-pmcp-claim-custom-extraction.md`: `extract_auth_from_proxy_headers` currently drops every `x-pmcp-claim-custom-*` header emitted by pmcp.run mcp-proxy, so `ctx.claim::<String>("custom:primary_creator")` always returns `None` even when the Cognito `custom:*` attribute was correctly forwarded.
